Privacy Policy


Last Updated: September 23, 2023


  1. Introduction: Welcome to Nearthy! We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy is designed to help you understand how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your data is handled responsibly. Please take a moment to read this Privacy Policy carefully to familiarize yourself with our practices. By using our mobile app, you consent to the terms described in this policy.


  2. Types of Data Collected: Nearthy collects the following types of data, which are stored locally on your device. We do not store your data in the cloud, and we do not own your information. The information we may request includes:

    - Name (Required): We ask for your name to personalize your Nearthy experience.

    - Profile Picture (Optional): You have the option to upload a profile picture to enhance your profile, but this is entirely voluntary.

    - Bio (Optional): You can provide a bio to share more about yourself, but it's also optional.

    - Contact Information (Only 1 required): You may choose to provide your phone number and/or Instagram username. You can provide one or both of these, depending on your preferences.

    - Location Data (Permission Required): Nearthy may request permission to access your device's location, but this is solely for the purpose of connecting with other devices via Bluetooth. We do not store your location data.


  3. How Data is Provided: Your data is provided to Nearthy during the creation of your user profile. The details you provide, including your name, profile picture, bio, and contact information (phone number and/or Instagram username), are voluntarily submitted by you when setting up your profile within the Nearthy app.
    Additionally, Nearthy may request permission to access your device's location for specific features, such as connecting with other devices via Bluetooth. This location data is solely used for the intended purpose and is not retained beyond the immediate use case.


  4. Purpose of Data Collection: Nearthy needs you to provide some information for its functionality. Nearthy can only work correctly if everybody has provided the required information. This information is essential for users to connect and interact with each other using Nearthy's nearby sharing features.


  5. Data Usage and Sharing: Nearthy exclusively uses your data to facilitate its core functionality, which is connecting you with nearby individuals. Here's how data usage and sharing work:

    - Service Activation: When you activate the Nearthy service within the app, Nearthy initiates a search for nearby individuals who have also activated the service. Only when a match is found will your data be shared.

    - Basic Information Sharing: Upon finding a match, Nearthy shares some information with the nearby user, including your name and profile picture. Simultaneously, you receive the same basic information from the other user and it can be seen on your main screen.

    - Request and Expanded Information Sharing: Once this basic information is visible on your main screen, you have the option to send or accept requests to and from other users. When a request is accepted, both users can see each other's full profile information, including name, profile picture (if provided), bio (if provided), and any contact information (Instagram username and/or phone number) the user has chosen to share. Also, Nearthy allows you to chat with the users while they are connected.

    Note: When a request is accepted and profiles are shared, the profiles will also be saved locally on each other's devices. This can be useful for the user and it also allows Nearthy to ignore users you have already met so you don't get useless notifications.

    Nearthy does not use your data for any purposes other than connecting you with nearby users and enhancing your interactions within the app. We do not share your data with third parties outside the scope of the app's functionality.


  6. User Control: Nearthy offers you control over your data and interactions within the app. Here's how you can manage your information:

    - Your Profile Information: Nearthy allows you to view and edit the information of your profile.

    - Data Deletion: You have full control over your data because it is stored in your device. You have the option to delete saved profiles individually within the app. This means you can remove profiles that you have interacted with and saved for future reference. You can also delete all your information from Nearthy in the device settings.


  7. Data Security: Nearthy prioritizes the security of your data. We want to assure you that we take advantage of the security measures provided by the Nearby Share functionality to safeguard your information. Nearby Share includes robust security features designed to protect data during sharing and interactions within the app.

    While we do not implement additional security measures beyond those provided by Nearby Share, we want to emphasize that your data security is of utmost importance to us. We continually monitor and update our app to ensure it aligns with the latest security practices and standards.

    Rest assured that we are committed to maintaining the confidentiality and integrity of your data as you use Nearthy.


  8. Data Retention: Nearthy retains user data to ensure the functionality of the app. Here's how data retention works:

    - Profile Information: Your profile information, including your name, profile picture, bio, and contact information, is stored on your device. You have the option to edit this information from the app, or delete all your information at any time from your app settings.

    - Saved Profiles: Profiles of other users you have interacted with and saved for future reference will be retained on your device. You can delete these profiles individually from inside the app.

    - Chat Conversations: Chat messages and conversations are stored on your device to facilitate ongoing interactions with other users. Please note that Nearthy currently offers the option to delete all data, including chat conversations, from the app settings. Individual chat message deletion is not available at this time.

    - Location Data: Nearthy may request permission to access your device's location for connecting with other devices via Bluetooth. Location data is not stored beyond the immediate use case and is not retained by the app.

    Please be aware that when you choose to delete data from your device through the app settings, it will result in the removal of all data. Nearthy is committed to respecting your privacy, and our data retention practices are designed to align with your preferences and the app's functionality.


  9. Third Party Links: Nearthy provides a feature that allows users to directly visit an Instagram profile from a username provided just by clicking on it. Here's what you need to know:

    - Instagram Integration: When you view a user's profile on Nearthy, you may have the option to click on their Instagram username. Clicking on this link will take you directly to the user's Instagram profile on the Instagram platform.

    - External Platform: Instagram is a third-party platform, and by clicking on an Instagram username within Nearthy, you will be redirected to the Instagram platform. Please note that your interactions on Instagram are subject to Instagram's terms of service and privacy policy.

    - Data Handling: When you visit an Instagram profile through Nearthy, Instagram may collect data about your interactions and activities as per its own privacy practices. Nearthy does not control or have access to the data collected by Instagram.

    - User Consent: Users who link their Instagram profiles to Nearthy profiles do so voluntarily and provide their consent for other users to visit their - Instagram profiles.

    It's important to understand that any interactions on external platforms, such as Instagram, are governed by the respective platform's terms of service and privacy policy. Nearthy does not have control over the data handling practices of these external platforms. Please review Instagram's privacy policy and terms of service to understand how your data is handled when you visit Instagram profiles.


  10. Updates to Privacy Policy: At Nearthy, we are committed to maintaining the transparency and security of your data. This commitment may lead us to periodically update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our data practices or legal requirements. Here's what you need to know about updates to this policy:

    - Notification of Changes: When we make updates to this Privacy Policy, we will revise the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document to indicate the date of the most recent changes. We encourage you to regularly review this policy to stay informed about how we collect, use, and protect your data.

    - Material Changes: In the event of material changes to this policy that significantly impact how your data is handled, we may provide additional notice through in-app notifications or email to ensure you are aware of and understand these changes.

    - Your Consent: By continuing to use Nearthy after updates to this Privacy Policy, you consent to the revised terms and practices. If you do not agree with the changes, you have the option to discontinue using the app.

    - Review Period: We recommend reviewing this Privacy Policy periodically to ensure that you are familiar with its contents and any updates that may have been made.


  11. Contact: If you have any questions, concerns, or requests related to your privacy or this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

    Email: contact@nearthy.com

    We value your privacy, and our team is here to assist you with any privacy-related inquiries or issues you may have.
